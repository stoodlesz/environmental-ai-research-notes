# About

This site is a place for research notes at the intersection of environmental geography, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity.

I am using it to document ideas, experiments, datasets, model tests, and reflections as the project develops. The tone is intentionally practical: part field notebook, part technical lab book, part public archive.

The first focus is coastal dune ecosystems and psammosere succession, with a particular interest in whether satellite imagery and computer vision can help monitor ecological change over time.

## Themes

- environmental monitoring
- satellite imagery
- computer vision
- secure machine learning
- dataset provenance
- model robustness
- restoration and ecosystem change
